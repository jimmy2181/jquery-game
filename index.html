Rules of the Game

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four characters below. By clicking on a character you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the room number in the Overlook Hotel, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random room number.

The value of each character is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each character.
